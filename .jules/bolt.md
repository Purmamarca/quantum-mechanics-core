## 2025-02-23 - Performance Optimization: Geometric Series for Lifetime Savings

**Learning:** Replaced an O(N) loop with a closed-form geometric series formula for calculating lifetime savings. While the loop was small (25 iterations), this change demonstrates how mathematical optimization can replace iterative computation, improving both efficiency and code elegance.
